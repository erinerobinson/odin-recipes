Egg Salad

This is supposed to be a recipe for egg salad sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs, hard boiled
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 1/2 tsp yellow mustard
    • 1 green onion 1 rib celery 2 tsp fresh dill

Steps

  1. Cut eggs in half. Remove yolks and chop whites.
    2. Mash yolks with mayo, mustard, salt, and pepper to taste until smooth and creamy
  2. Add remaining ingredients and stir well
  3. Serve on bread over lettuce
