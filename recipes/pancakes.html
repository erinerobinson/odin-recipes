Pancakes

This is supposed to be a recipe for pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugat
  • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp salt 1 egg 1 cup milk 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approx 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot
