Tacos

This is supposed to be a recipe for taco

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
    • 3/4 tsp salt 1/2 dried oregano 1/2 tsp garlic powder 1/4 tsp ground black pepper 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • some of them taco shells

Steps

  1. Add beef to a large skillet over medium high heat. Break meat apart with a wooden spoon. Add spices to meat and stir well. Cook until the meat is cooked through, about 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally
  2. Reduce heat to medium. Add tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine. Cook, stirring occasionally for 7-8 minutes, until some of the liquid evaporates but the meat mixture is still a little saucy remove from heat
  3. Warm the tacos
  4. Fill them tacos
